# Product

<!-- impeccable:product-schema 1 -->

## Platform

web

## Users

Primary: Indian students (age 15–30) studying computer science, programming, typing, graphic design, video editing, accounting, and competitive exams (SSC, banking, etc.). Secondary: working professionals looking to upskill in software and AI tools.

## Product Purpose

Payal Education Society Computers is a premium digital-first learning platform that provides hand-curated educational resources for computer training and related fields. Students purchase access to structured content including lectures, PDFs, assignments, and practice files.

## Positioning

A local institution with premium digital packaging: expert-curated, specific to Indian competitive and vocational curricula, with lifetime access and no subscription fatigue.

## Operating Context

Students access the platform on desktop and mobile, often in lower-bandwidth environments. Many are first-time online learners. Content is primarily consumed, not created.

## Capabilities and Constraints

- Resource browsing and purchase
- Secure in-browser streaming of lecture content
- PDF and file downloads
- Blog and resource pages
- Admin portal
- Supabase backend (auth, database, storage)
- Deployed on Cloudflare (cloudflare-module preset)
- TanStack Start (SSR React router framework)
- Tailwind v4 + framer-motion + Three.js WebGL backgrounds

## Brand Commitments

- Name: Payal Education Society Computers
- Tone: serious but approachable, trustworthy, premium
- Dark-first interface (near-black background)
- Geist Variable as the typeface (already installed)

## Evidence on Hand

- Full working codebase at src/
- FloatingLines, DotField, Particles, ShapeGrid WebGL components available
- All pages built: home, resources, blog, about, contact, auth, admin

## Product Principles

1. Quality over quantity — curated resources, no filler
2. Permanent access — ownership model, not subscription
3. Curriculum-aligned — content matches Indian vocational and competitive exam needs
4. Premium digital feel — punches above local competitors
5. Accessible first — works on mobile and low-bandwidth
